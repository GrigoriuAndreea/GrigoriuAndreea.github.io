Andreea Grigoriu is a Ph.D. candidate in Digital Legal Studies who joined the Law and Tech Lab, part of the Faculty of Law.

She obtained her Msc in Artificial Intelligence degree at the Department of Data Science and Knowledge Engineering,  Faculty of Science and Engineering, at Maastricht University. Both her Internship and Master Thesis were completed at the [Institute of Data Science](https://www.maastrichtuniversity.nl/research/institute-data-science) with a general focus on Semantic Enhancement of data using a combination of Machine Learning, Semantic Technologies (Knowledge Graphs, Ontologies) and Text Mining and NLP techniques.


As a PhD candidate, she is working on understanding the phenomena of online harassment on social media, specifically abusive language using both legal research and data science computational methods.
